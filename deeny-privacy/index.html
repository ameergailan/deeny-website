Deeny Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-12-31

Deeny ("App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services. By accessing or using Deeny, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected during account creation for communication and authentication purposes.
  • Location Data: Used to provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • Sign-In Information: Includes username and password for account access.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Such as device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Includes interaction with features, viewed content, and app performance metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Prayer Times and Qibla: To provide personalized and accurate prayer timings based on your location.
  • Account Management: To authenticate users and communicate important updates.
  • Improvement: To enhance app features, functionality, and user experience.
  • Content Moderation: To ensure user-generated content complies with Islamic guidelines and App rules.

3. Sharing of Information

Deeny does not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services (e.g., analytics, hosting) to support App operations.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court orders, or government regulations.

4. Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted during transmission.
  • Secure Servers: Your data is stored on servers with restricted access.
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews to maintain compliance.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. Users share information at their own risk.

5. Your Rights

As a Deeny user, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (may limit App functionality).

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@deeny.app

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained for the duration of your use of the App.
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal information is deleted or anonymized within 30 days of account deletion unless required by law.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Deeny may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve app performance.
  • Analyze user behavior for feature enhancement.

You can disable cookies through your device settings; however, this may impact functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Deeny does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians with concerns can contact us directly.

9. International Users

If you access Deeny from outside Canada, you agree to the transfer and processing of your data in Canada. We comply with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR for users in the European Union.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or through the App. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at: support@deeny.app

Thank you for trusting Deeny with your privacy. Your data security and compliance with Islamic principles are our priority.
